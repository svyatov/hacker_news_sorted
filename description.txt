Sort Hacker News your way — by points, time, or comments — instantly.

HN's default ranking is great, but sometimes you want something different. Want the most upvoted stories? Sort by points. Looking for fresh posts? Sort by time. Curious what everyone's talking about? Sort by comments. One click and the page reorders — no reload, no new tab.

The sort controls sit right in HN's header bar. Pick a sort, and your preference sticks across sessions and devices. Come back tomorrow and it remembers.

New post indicators mark stories that appeared since your last visit with an orange dot, so you never re-scan the same front page twice. Toggle them on or off from the extension popup.

Keyboard shortcuts: P for points, T for time, C for comments, D for default. If you use Vimium, the shortcuts step aside automatically.

Works on every HN page with a post list — front page, Newest, Ask, Show, Jobs, and more.

No data collection. No tracking. Runs entirely in your browser.
